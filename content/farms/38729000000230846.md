Carr Family Christmas Tree Farm is owned and operated by the Carr family in Guysborough. The farm offers a variety of Christmas tree species, including Balsam Fir, Scotch Pine, White Pine, and White and Norway Spruce. Customers can choose to cut their own tree or select from a selection of pre-cut trees. The farm features a scenic, rural setting with a pleasant walk through the woods to reach the trees. After selecting and cutting their tree, visitors can warm up by a cozy bonfire on the premises. The Carr Family Christmas Tree Farm provides a peaceful, non-commercial holiday experience for families to create cherished memories.
